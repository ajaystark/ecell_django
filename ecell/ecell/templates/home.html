Design Thinking Workshop
Click Here
22/07/19

Design Thinking is a 5 step process to come up with creative solutions to solve real-world problems for a particular group of people.
Step 1: Explore and Understand
Think about various situations in the problem sector and identify the group of people/communities being affected. Understand their problems specific to that field. Make use of any reliable resource (Books, Internet, People, Newspapers etc.) to gather information for a deeper analysis of the situation.
Step 2: Define Problem Statement
After the complete in-depth analysis of different problem scenarios, come up with a definitive problem statement which clearly states what problem you are explicitly focusing on and who are the stakeholders.
Step 3: Ideate
Now focus only on the problem statement you've formed, and come up with creative and unique idea to solve the problem. Best ideations occur when you think as a team and critique each other's ideas. You can propose a solution in any form - a hardware tool, an application, a website, or maybe something new!
Step 4: Prototype
Before the final approval of an idea, it is important to brainstorm and verify it's functionality as per the problem statement. Take a moment to reflect on what you've learnt from your discussions and brainstorming. Connect the dots and sketch a basic prototype of your solution proposal on paper.
Step 5: Evaluate
Evaluate your prototype by testing it on diverse groups of people to get feedback and critiques for further improvement.
